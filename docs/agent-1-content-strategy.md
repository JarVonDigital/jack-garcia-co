## Global Messaging Direction

- Lead with Jack Garcia Co. as a warm, editorial Florida photographer, not as an Instagram profile.
- Keep Instagram as supporting proof of recent work, not as the primary identity or first conversion driver.
- Make inquiry the clearest next step across all primary pages.
- Use service naming consistently: Weddings, Engagement, Families, Graduation, Couple, and Other.
- Remove Process from the main navigation, homepage structure, and any top-level CTA path.

## Navigation Model

- Primary navigation: Home, Services, About, Reviews, Inquire.
- Persistent CTA label: Inquire.
- Secondary links can include Instagram, privacy, and terms in the footer.
- Do not feature follower count, post count, or highlight count in navigation or hero-adjacent UI.

## Homepage Purpose

The Home page should introduce the brand, clarify the primary offers, build trust quickly, and direct visitors deeper into Services, About, Reviews, or Inquire.

### Homepage Content Order

1. Hero
2. Signature services overview
3. Featured work preview
4. About preview
5. Reviews preview
6. Inquiry CTA band

### Homepage Content Details

#### 1. Hero

Keep:

- Clear brand statement focused on photography experience and style.
- One supporting sentence about Jack's approach, location, and availability.
- Primary CTA to Inquire.
- Secondary CTA to Services or View Offerings.
- Editorial imagery.

Remove or reduce:

- Instagram profile framing.
- Follower, post, or highlight metrics.
- Language that reads like a social bio.
- Any "DM to inquire" phrasing if a dedicated inquiry path exists.

#### 2. Signature Services Overview

Include:

- Weddings
- Engagement
- Families
- Graduation
- Couple
- Short one-line value statement for each.
- A single pathway to the Services page for full details.

Do not include:

- Full long-form pricing tables.
- Secondary niche offerings inline with the primary categories.

#### 3. Featured Work Preview

Include:

- A curated visual preview that reinforces style and subject range.
- Light copy connecting the work to real client experiences.

Keep out of Home if possible:

- Large Instagram grids.
- Social UI conventions like tabs, post chrome, or profile headers.

#### 4. About Preview

Include:

- A short personal intro to Jack.
- One image placeholder for portrait/headshot.
- Link to the About page.

#### 5. Reviews Preview

Include:

- A short trust statement.
- One to three testimonial placeholders clearly marked as pending client quotes.
- Link to the Reviews page.

#### 6. Inquiry CTA Band

Include:

- Clear invitation to reach out.
- Brief reassurance on what happens after inquiry.
- Single CTA to the Inquire page or form.

## What Should Move Off Home

Move these items to dedicated pages or reduce significantly:

- Full About narrative.
- Full service pricing breakdowns.
- Full Instagram/profile section.
- Any Process timeline.
- Long recent highlights carousel content.
- Any legal or utility content beyond normal footer behavior.

## Services Page Purpose

The Services page should help visitors self-identify their session type, understand what Jack prioritizes, and move closer to inquiry.

### Services Page Content Order

1. Services intro
2. Primary category navigation or tabs
3. Wedding packages
4. Non-wedding categories
5. Other offerings
6. Inquiry CTA

### Service Labels

Use these labels consistently in navigation, headings, cards, and CTA copy:

- Weddings
- Engagement
- Families
- Graduation
- Couple
- Other

### Primary Categories

- Weddings: highest-priority service, presented first and with the most detail.
- Engagement: distinct from Couple if Jack wants both session types separated.
- Families: family sessions.
- Graduation: graduate and senior-focused sessions if that matches his booking mix.
- Couple: non-engagement couples sessions.

### Other Grouping

Move the following into Other unless Jack says one should be elevated:

- Maternity
- Branding
- Baby / Newborn
- Automotive
- Portraits

### Known Non-Wedding Starting Prices

Based on `Services/Pricing.md`:

- Graduation - $200
- Portraits - $180
- Family - $250
- Couple - $200
- Engagement - $225
- Maternity - $200
- Branding - $200
- Baby / Newborn - $300
- Automotive - $150

### Wedding Package Structure

Weddings should not use generic custom-coverage language as the main presentation. The page should reserve space for:

- Package name
- Starting price
- Coverage hours
- Number of photographers
- Engagement session included or not
- Estimated gallery size or image count
- Sneak peeks or preview timeline
- Final delivery timeline
- Printing rights
- Travel coverage details
- Add-ons
- Overtime policy
- Booking retainer / payment structure

## About Page Purpose

The About page should make Jack feel personal, credible, and memorable.

### About Page Content Order

1. Intro headline
2. Portrait/headshot area
3. Personal story
4. Approach and client experience
5. Short facts or values
6. Inquiry CTA

### About Content Direction

Include:

- Who Jack is.
- Where he is based.
- How he approaches photographing people and moments.
- What kinds of clients or stories he loves documenting.
- What makes the experience feel calm, warm, and personal.

Avoid:

- Generic photographer cliches.
- Placeholder biography written as fact.
- Overemphasis on social media growth or creator metrics.

## Reviews Page Purpose

The Reviews page should centralize trust signals and let future clients see what it feels like to work with Jack.

### Reviews Page Content Order

1. Trust-focused intro
2. Testimonial grid or stacked cards
3. Optional service-type filters later
4. Inquiry CTA

### Reviews Content Direction

Include:

- Real client quotes only.
- Client first names, session type, and optional location if approved.
- Clean placeholders labeled as pending until testimonials are received.

Avoid:

- Fabricated testimonials.
- Anonymous quotes presented as final if no approval exists.

## Inquire Page Purpose

The Inquire page should be the clearest conversion path and replace the old Process-first framing.

### Inquire Page Content Order

1. Simple welcome copy
2. Preferred contact method or inquiry form
3. What to share in the inquiry
4. Expected response timing
5. Optional FAQ or note on availability

### Inquiry Messaging Direction

Use inquiry-first language such as:

- Inquire
- Start your inquiry
- Share your date or session idea
- Tell Jack what you are planning
- Ask about availability

Avoid:

- Book now if the process is still conversational.
- DM-first language unless Instagram remains a confirmed inquiry channel.

## Copy Consistency Guidance

### Brand Positioning

Use language that emphasizes:

- Warm
- Editorial
- Timeless
- Personal
- Florida-based
- Available for travel

### Service Naming Rules

- Use `Couple` only if Jack confirms he prefers that wording publicly.
- If he prefers standard usage, update public-facing copy to `Couples` everywhere.
- Use `Families` instead of `Family` for category labels.
- Use `Other` as the umbrella heading for secondary services.

### CTA Rules

Primary CTA across pages:

- Inquire

Secondary CTA options:

- View services
- Learn more about Jack
- Read reviews

### Instagram Positioning Rules

- Refer to Instagram as recent work or social proof.
- Place it below the primary brand story and service explanation.
- Never let Instagram UI patterns define the hero or top-of-page structure.
